Thank You For Being Here

For that reason, have a poem-



In the garden of life, we each take our turn,

Blossoming brightly, then starting to yearn.

For seasons they change, and time marches on,

In the blink of an eye, from dusk until dawn.


The laughter we've shared, the tears we've shed,

In the book of our memories, they're lovingly spread.

Friendships and moments, like stars in the night,

They twinkle and shimmer, a beautiful sight.


So let's cherish this journey, wherever it leads,

With courage and grace for all of our needs.

For life is a canvas, and we are the art,

Painting our stories from the depths of the heart.


As chapters conclude, and new ones begin,

May happiness find you, and peace from within.

Though our paths may diverge, and distances grow,

In spirit, we'll stay connected, this I surely know.
